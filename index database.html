Create a new account.

Hi! For full access to courses you'll need to take a minute to create a new account on this web site. Here are the steps:

  1. Fill out the New Account form with your details ensuring your username is your hackney.gov.uk account. 
  2. This will enable us to link your completed courses to your learning record in iTrent. If you do not have a hackney.gov.uk email address you may use an alternative email.
  3. An email will be immediately sent to your email address.
  4. Read your email, and click on the link it contains.
  5. Your account will be confirmed and you will be logged in.
  6. Now, select the course you want to participate in.
