Course Overview

Trauma can leave wounds that affect the heart, mind, body, relationships, and spiritual life. This course helps you understand trauma through biblical wisdom, prayer, reflection, and practical healing principles rooted in Christ.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand trauma and its effects
  • Recognise emotional triggers
  • Process grief and loss biblically
  • Renew your mind through Scripture
  • Develop healing prayer practices
  • Build resilience and hope
  • Restore relationships
  • Create a long-term healing plan
