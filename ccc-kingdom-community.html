FAQ
Questions you might be asking
If you’re discerning with Jesus, that’s a good sign. Here’s some clarity for the journey.
  • ❓ What if I can’t make every live session?
    All live sessions are recorded and uploaded within 24 hours. Many past participants have journeyed primarily via replays and still experienced deep transformation—especially when they engaged in the community space.
  • ❓ Is this for pastors and leaders only?
    No. Some are pastors and ministry leaders, but many are teachers, creatives, entrepreneurs, parents, students, and professionals. The common thread is hunger for Jesus and a desire to see His Kingdom expressed in everyday life.
  • ❓ What if money is a real barrier right now?
    We never want finances to be the only reason someone can’t say yes. We offer limited scholarships and payment plans each cohort. When enrollment opens, you’ll be able to indicate if you need support and we’ll prayerfully review each request.
  • ❓ Which denomination or stream is this connected to?
    We’re unapologetically Jesus‑centered, Bible‑honoring, Spirit‑empowered, and Kingdom‑focused. We welcome believers from many streams who are willing to honor one another and keep Jesus at the center.
  • ❓ How do I join the next cohort?
    When enrollment opens, you’ll receive an invitation with dates, times, and a secure checkout link. Use the “Get notified” button above to make sure you don’t miss it.
